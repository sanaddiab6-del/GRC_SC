## Section 1: Policy Framework Overview (6 minutes)

### What is a Policy?

A policy is a formal rule approved by management that defines required behavior and controls. Policies are mandatory, not optional.

**SICO Policy Structure:**
- **Master Policy**: POL-IS-001 (Information Security Policy)
- **Topic Policies**: 44 policies across 15 categories
- **Procedures**: Step-by-step instructions that implement policy
- **Standards**: Technical requirements (e.g., password length)

### Where to Find Policies

- SICO GRC Policy Library: `https://grc.sicocompany.sa/policies`
- Company Intranet: `https://intranet.sicocompany.sa/security`
- Policy acknowledgements tracked in the ISMS module

### Mandatory Policies (Must Read and Acknowledge)

1. POL-IS-001 Information Security Policy
2. POL-AC-001 Access Control Policy
3. POL-AC-002 Password Policy
4. POL-HR-002 Security Awareness and Training Policy

## Section 2: Key Policies and What They Require (10 minutes)

### Access Control (POL-AC-001)

**You must:**
- Use your own account only (no shared accounts)
- Request access through approved workflow
- Use least privilege (only what you need)
- Complete quarterly access reviews (managers)

**Never:**
- Share passwords or MFA tokens
- Use someone else's badge or account
- Bypass approval processes

### Password Policy (POL-AC-002)

**Requirements:**
- 12+ characters (14 for admins)
- MFA required for all accounts
- Use 1Password Enterprise (mandatory)
- Change every 90 days (60 for admins)

### Incident Response (POL-IM-001)

**Report all incidents immediately (within 1 hour)**
- Email: security@sicocompany.sa
- Hotline: +966-XX-XXXX-XXXX (24/7)
- Portal: https://security.sicocompany.sa/report

### Business Continuity (POL-BC-001)

**Key points:**
- RTO = 4 hours, RPO = 1 hour
- Follow DR procedures during outages
- Participate in annual DR tests if requested

### Compliance Management (POL-CM-001)

**Your responsibilities:**
- Follow all security policies
- Complete mandatory training
- Cooperate with audits
- Report compliance concerns

## Section 3: Policy Acknowledgement Process (5 minutes)

### How to Acknowledge Policies

1. Login to the SICO GRC Platform
2. Go to **Policies > Acknowledgements**
3. Review assigned policies
4. Click **Acknowledge** and confirm understanding
5. Completion recorded in `policy_acknowledgements`

### When Acknowledgement is Required

- New hire onboarding (within 5 business days)
- Annual policy refresh (every 12 months)
- After major policy updates

### Consequences for Non-Acknowledgement

- Automated reminders at 7, 14, 21 days
- Manager notified after 30 days
- Account access suspended after 60 days

---

## Section 4: Policy Violations and Disciplinary Actions (5 minutes)

### What is a Policy Violation?

Any failure to comply with security policies or procedures.

**Examples:**
- Sharing passwords
- Downloading customer data to personal devices
- Ignoring incident reporting requirements
- Disabling security tools (antivirus, MFA)
- Allowing tailgating into secure areas

### Disciplinary Process (POL-HR-003)

1. **Verbal Warning** (minor first-time violation)
2. **Written Warning** (repeat violation)
3. **Suspension** (serious violation)
4. **Termination** (gross misconduct, intentional breach)

**Note:** Serious violations may also lead to legal action and regulatory reporting.
