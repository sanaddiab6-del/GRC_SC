# PDPL Compliance Matrix

## Overview

This matrix maps SICO's compliance with Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). SICO processes customer and employee personal data and maintains full compliance.

**Compliance Status**: 100% for applicable PDPL articles

**Regulatory Authority**: Saudi Data & AI Authority (SDAIA)

---

## Key PDPL Articles and Implementation

| **PDPL Article** | **Requirement** | **Status** | **Evidence** |
|------------------|-----------------|-----------|--------------|
| Article 3 | Lawful basis for processing | ✅ Implemented | Consent records, contractual necessity, legal obligations documented |
| Article 4 | Purpose limitation | ✅ Implemented | Data processing registers, documented purposes |
| Article 5 | Data minimization | ✅ Implemented | Collection forms limited to necessary fields |
| Article 6 | Data accuracy | ✅ Implemented | Data correction workflows, DSAR procedures |
| Article 7 | Staff training | ✅ Implemented | Mandatory training modules, HR tracking |
| Article 8 | Confidentiality | ✅ Implemented | NDAs, access controls, encryption |
| Article 9 | Cross-border transfers | ✅ Implemented | Data residency in Saudi regions, SCCs if needed |
| Article 10 | Data retention | ✅ Implemented | Retention schedules, automated deletion jobs |
| Article 11 | Contracts and confidentiality | ✅ Implemented | HR contracts, vendor DPAs |
| Article 12 | Data subject rights | ✅ Implemented | DSAR process (30 days), privacy portal |
| Article 13 | Security measures | ✅ Implemented | ISO 27001 controls, encryption, monitoring |
| Article 14 | Breach notification | ✅ Implemented | PDPL breach response within 72 hours |
| Article 20 | SDAIA notification | ✅ Implemented | DPO notification procedure, incident playbooks |

---

## Data Subject Rights (DSAR)

SICO supports all PDPL rights:
- Access
- Correction
- Deletion (with legal exceptions)
- Restriction/objection
- Portability
- Withdrawal of consent

Requests are handled by the DPO at privacy@sicocompany.sa within 30 days.

## Compliance Statement

SICO maintains full PDPL compliance through policy enforcement, technical controls, staff training, and continuous monitoring.
