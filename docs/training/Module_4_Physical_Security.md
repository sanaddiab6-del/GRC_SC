## Section 1: Access Control (5 minutes)

### Badge Access System

**Your Badge:**
- Photo ID badge with RFID chip
- Wear visibly above waist (front or lanyard)
- Required for all entry points (main entrance, server room, parking)

**DO:**
- ✅ Swipe your own badge every time
- ✅ Wait for door to close behind you
- ✅ Challenge unescorted visitors politely: "Hi, can I help you?"
- ✅ Report lost badge immediately: security@sicocompany.sa or +966-XX-XXXX-XXXX

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Lend badge to anyone (even colleagues who "forgot" theirs)
- ❌ Hold doors for people without badges (tailgating)
- ❌ Prop doors open (fire code violation + security risk)
- ❌ Use others' badges

**Lost Badge:** Report immediately → Badge deactivated within 15 minutes → Replacement issued next business day (SAR 100 fee)

### Tailgating Prevention

**What is Tailgating?**
Following an authorized person through a secure door without using your own badge.

**Why It's Dangerous:**
- Bypasses access controls and logging
- Unknown people enter secure areas
- Common tactic in physical social engineering

**How to Prevent:**
1. **One person, one badge swipe** (even if multiple people)
2. Close door completely before next person enters
3. Politely ask tailgaters to use their badge: "Sorry, everyone needs to badge in for security"

**Escalation:** If someone refuses to badge or acts suspiciously → Don't confront → Call security: ext. 9999 or security@sicocompany.sa

### Visitor Management

**All Visitors Must:**
1. Sign in at reception (provide ID, company, purpose)
2. Receive temporary visitor badge (must be worn visibly)
3. Be escorted at all times by SICO employee (sponsor)
4. Sign out and return badge when leaving

**Your Responsibilities as Visitor Sponsor:**
- ✅ Submit visitor request 24 hours in advance (security clearance)
- ✅ Meet visitor at reception
- ✅ Escort to meeting room/office (no unsupervised wandering)
- ✅ Prevent access to sensitive areas (server room, other departments)
- ✅ Ensure sign-out when leaving

**Challenge Unknown People:**
If you see someone without a badge or unescorted visitor:
- Approach politely: "Hi, can I help you find someone?"
- If badge-less: "Do you have a visitor badge? I can escort you to reception."
- If suspicious behavior: Don't confront → Call security extension 9999

## Section 2: Clean Desk & Clear Screen (4 minutes)

### Clean Desk Policy (POL-PS-002)

**When Leaving Your Desk (Even 5 Minutes):**

**DO:**
- ✅ Lock computer screen (Windows + L or Ctrl+Alt+Delete → Lock)
- ✅ Lock confidential documents in drawer/cabinet
- ✅ Turn monitor away from walkways/windows
- ✅ Log out of applications with sensitive data
- ✅ Use shredder for disposal (not recycling bin)

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Leave printed reports on desk (especially Confidential/Restricted)
- ❌ Sticky notes with passwords on monitor
- ❌ Customer data visible on screen when away
- ❌ USB drives lying around

**Automatic Screen Lock:**
- Configured: 10 minutes idle → Lock automatically
- Best practice: Lock manually when leaving (don't wait 10 min)

### Printer/Copier Security

**Problem:** Sensitive documents left on printer → Anyone can view

**Solution:**
- ✅ Retrieve printouts immediately (don't send and walk away)
- ✅ Use pull printing (swipe badge at printer to release job)
- ✅ Verify no documents left behind (check output tray + scanner glass)
- ✅ Shred mistakes/drafts (don't throw in trash)

**Found Unclaimed Documents?**
- Deliver to document owner if known
- Shred if unattended >1 hour or classified Confidential+
- Report repeated violations to owner's manager

### Whiteboards and Brainstorming

**After Meetings:**
- ✅ Photograph whiteboard notes (if needed for project)
- ✅ Erase completely (especially in shared conference rooms)
- ✅ Don't leave flipcharts with confidential data

**Video Calls:**
- ✅ Check camera background (no sensitive documents visible on desk/whiteboard)
- ✅ Use blurred background if working from home

## Section 3: Mobile Device Security (4 minutes)

### Laptop Security

**Physical Protection:**
- ✅ Use cable lock in public spaces (library, café, airport)
- ✅ Never leave unattended (even for 30 seconds to use restroom)
- ✅ Keep in locked car trunk if left in vehicle (not visible on seat)
- ✅ Laptop bag at your feet during transport (not on floor where you can't see)

**Technical Protection (Already Configured):**
- ✅ BitLocker disk encryption (automatic on company laptops)
- ✅ Windows Defender for Endpoint (EDR)
- ✅ Strong password + fingerprint/face unlock
- ✅ Remote wipe capability (if lost, IT can erase remotely)

**Lost/Stolen:** Report immediately +966-XX-XXXX-XXXX (24/7 hotline) → Remote wipe activated within 1 hour

### Smartphone/Tablet Security

**SICO Policy:**
- Company-issued phones: Enrolled in Microsoft Intune (MDM)
- Personal phones: Can access work email only if enrolled
- **NO** accessing company data on unenrolled personal devices

**Required Settings:**
- ✅ 6-digit PIN minimum (or biometric)
- ✅ Auto-lock after 5 minutes idle
- ✅ Encryption enabled (automatic on modern devices)
- ✅ Remote wipe capability

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Download company files to personal devices
- ❌ Use public charging stations (USB data theft risk) → Use AC adapter
- ❌ Jailbreak/root devices (disables security)

### USB Drives and External Media

**SICO Policy:**
- ✅ Use only SICO-issued encrypted USB drives (IronKey or similar)
- ❌ Personal USB drives prohibited on company computers (malware risk)
- ✅ Scan all external media with antivirus before opening files

**Found USB Drive?**
- ❌ **NEVER plug into computer** (could be malware, "baiting" attack)
- ✅ Turn in to security/IT (they have isolated analysis system)

## Section 4: Incident Reporting (2 minutes)

### Physical Security Incidents

**Report Immediately:**
- 🚨 Lost badge, laptop, phone, or USB drive
- 🚨 Unauthorized person in secure area
- 🚨 Propped/damaged doors (security breach)
- 🚨 Suspicious activity (photography of server room, tailgating attempts)
- 🚨 Theft or vandalism
- 🚨 Found unattended confidential documents

**How to Report:**
- Emergency: ext. 9999 (security desk)
- Non-emergency: security@sicocompany.sa
- 24/7 hotline: +966-XX-XXXX-XXXX

**What Happens:**
- Security Team responds within 15 minutes (emergencies)
- Investigation conducted
- Lost devices remotely wiped
- Badges deactivated
- CCTV footage reviewed
