## Data Subject Request Workflows

### Access Request (Article 4)
1. Receive request (form: `dsar_request_form.docx`)
2. Verify identity (process: `identity_verification_procedure.docx`)
3. Search systems (template: `data_search_log.xlsx`)
4. Compile data (format: `data_export_template.xlsx`)
5. Deliver within 30 days (tracking: `dsar_tracking_log.xlsx`)

### Erasure Request (Article 7)
1. Receive deletion request
2. Validate grounds for erasure
3. Identify all data locations
4. Execute deletion (log: `erasure_log.xlsx`)
5. Confirm completion (letter: `erasure_confirmation.docx`)
6. Notify third parties if data was shared

### Objection Request (Article 10)
1. Receive objection
2. Assess legal basis for processing
3. Cease processing or provide compelling grounds
4. Document decision (form: `objection_decision.docx`)

## Bilingual Requirements

All customer-facing documents must be in Arabic and English:
- ✅ Privacy Policy
- ✅ Consent Forms
- ✅ DSAR Response Letters
- ✅ Data Breach Notifications to Individuals
- ✅ Privacy Notices

Internal documents may be English-only:
- RoPA entries
- PIAs
- Internal procedures
- Training materials (unless for Arabic-speaking staff)

## Evidence Retention

Per PDPL requirements:
- **Consent records**: Duration of processing + 2 years
- **DSAR logs**: 5 years
- **Breach records**: 7 years
- **PIA documents**: 3 years after project completion
- **RoPA entries**: Current + 3 years after processing ends

## Common Evidence Gaps (Audit Findings)

### High-Risk Gaps
1. ❌ No documented legal basis for processing
2. ❌ Missing consent for special categories of data
3. ❌ No data breach notification procedures
4. ❌ No DPO appointed

### Medium-Risk Gaps
1. ⚠️ RoPA incomplete or outdated
2. ⚠️ Privacy policy not in Arabic
3. ⚠️ No PIA for high-risk processing
4. ⚠️ DSAR process not tested

### Remediation Priority
1. Appoint DPO (mandatory)
2. Create RoPA (mandatory)
3. Obtain valid consents
4. Implement breach procedures
5. Conduct PIAs for high-risk activities
